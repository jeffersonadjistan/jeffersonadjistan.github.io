Bored

Everyone feels bored sometimes, it's that slow moment when life seems to pause. But boredom isn't always bad. It can be a quiet nudge to slow down, notice your thoughts, or make space for something new. Sometimes, God uses still moments to speak, refresh your heart, or guide you toward creativity and purpose.

"Whatever you do, work at it with all your heart, as working for the Lord." , Colossians 3:23

This verse reminds us that every moment, even the quiet ones, can have meaning. God invites us to do all things with heart and purpose, whether they feel exciting or ordinary. Boredom can be a doorway to creativity, reflection, or prayer. It's not wasted time, but a pause that prepares you for what's next.
