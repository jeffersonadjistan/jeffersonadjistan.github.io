Joy

Joy is not only just a happy moment, but also embracing the bright side of life, choosing gratitude even when things aren't perfect, and recognizing the beauty in small blessings. It can be found in laughter with friends, in quiet peace after prayer, and in knowing that every good thing comes from God. Real joy doesn't fade when challenges come, rather it stays rooted in faith, hope, and love.

“The joy of the Lord is your strength.” — Nehemiah 8:10

This verse reminds us that true joy doesn’t depend on what’s happening around us — it comes from knowing God’s love and presence. When we focus on His goodness, that joy becomes our strength. It lifts us up, steadies our hearts, and fills us with peace even in uncertain times.

Here are a few ideas:

  • 🌞 Go outside and enjoy the nature
  • 🎵 Listen to your favorite upbeat song
  • 📞 Call someone you love and talk
  • 🎨 Do something creative and fun
  • 🙏 Take a moment to thank God for your blessings
